Help impacted people by GoFund

After a devastating flood in early June that affected around 4 million residents, the same area was hit again with record-breaking rainfall that began on June 15. Most of the Sylhet city has gone underwater again, with water continuing to rise, worsening the condition of those still living in temporary shelters and further exposed to extreme weather and waterborne diseases. Most of the affected communities are the ones that are already living in very poor conditions and low economy households. Floods can harm communities by destroying homes, damaging transportation, and producing outages and landslides. Support those who have been impacted by a flood by donating to their fundraiser.
